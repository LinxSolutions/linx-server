About Us.

Our Company

Linx aims to disrupt the language learning space by offering a unique learning experience when attempting to learn a new language. What started out as a 24 hour hackathon project during the Global Hackathon Series in 2017, Linx was developed by a team of 4 software engineers in New York City in the span of 12 weeks as park of AngelHack's pre-accelterator program.
