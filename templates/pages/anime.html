How it Works


1
Install Linx Chrome Extention
Once installed, you will be automatically directed to a sign up form where you can describe your skill level and preferences.
2

Linx generates a custom curriculum and roadmap for you and changes as you keep watching more anime.
3
Watch and Read Subtitles
Tranlation and transliteration will accompany key words and phrases and will be highlighted for you.

4
Take a Quiz After Every Episode
Quizzes will be based on highlighted words and phrases as well as grammer. Earn satisfactory scores on quizzes to level up.
5
Track Your Progress
Gain insight into your strengths and weaknesses and compare your progress with your friends and the community.
